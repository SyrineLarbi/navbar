Welcome to Jumia

Jumia Marketplace offers goods from a large and diverse group of sellers across a wide range of categories including apparel, smartphones, electronics, homeware and beauty. Jumia also provides consumers with easy access to a number of services, such as restaurant food delivery and every-day digital services including airtime recharge, utility bills payment, and transport ticketing.

Jumia Logistics facilitates the convenient and reliable delivery of goods. It consists of a large network of leased warehouses, pick-up stations for consumers, and drop off locations for sellers and more than 700 third-party logistics service providers, whom we integrate and manage through our proprietary technology, data, and processes.

JumiaPay is our very own payment service, designed to facilitate online transactions between sellers and consumers and provide certain participants with access to financial services. Our JumiaPay app also allows consumers to complete online payments, such as airtime recharge or utility payments and provides our sellers with access to attractive financing solutions offered by our financial partners.

JForce is a network of sales consultants that place Jumia platform orders on behalf of consumers in a commission based program. Our objective of this network is to overcome e-commerce's main challenges such as limited internet access and low trust in e-commerce while, at the same time, empowering entrepreneurs and generating employment through Jumia's established brand. Jumia is taking Africa’s economies online, helping small and large businesses grow and reach new consumers. In 2021 we’ve partnered with over 100,000 sellers, enabling them to attract millions of consumers towards their products.
